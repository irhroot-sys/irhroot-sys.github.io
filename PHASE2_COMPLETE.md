```html
<div class="animate-fade-up">

</div>
```

#### Staggered Fade In
```html
<div class="animate-fade-up animate-fade-up-delay-1">Item 1</div>
<div class="animate-fade-up animate-fade-up-delay-2">Item 2</div>
<div class="animate-fade-up animate-fade-up-delay-3">Item 3</div>
```
