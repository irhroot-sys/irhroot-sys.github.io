About Us

Amanat Al-Kalima Company

Amanat Al-Kalima Company is a licensed scrap metal trading company headquartered in Dammam, Eastern Province, Saudi Arabia. Established in 2017, we deliver complete end-to-end metal trading solutions — including ferrous and non-ferrous scrap buying, selling, collection, sorting, and recycling. We serve industrial plants, factories, construction contractors, demolition companies, and individual clients across the Eastern Province with transparent pricing, prompt pickup, and fully professional service standards.

What We Do

Core Services

Active across all key areas of the scrap trading value chain, serving businesses and individuals.

01

Scrap Metal Buying

We purchase all types of ferrous and non-ferrous scrap metal at competitive, transparent market prices. Serving factories, industrial facilities, demolition companies, and individuals across Dammam and the Eastern Province.

02

Scrap Metal Selling

We supply quality sorted scrap metal to manufacturers, steel mills, smelters, and businesses requiring specific metal types and grades. Reliable supply with consistent quality.

03

Scrap Collection & Pickup

On-site scrap metal collection service from your factory, construction site, or industrial facility across Dammam, Al-Khobar, Jubail, Dhahran, and Al Qatif. Fast scheduling, professional team.

04

Ferrous Metal Trading

Specialized trading in all iron and steel scrap including structural steel, reinforcement bars, industrial machinery scrap, and pressed steel — fully sorted and graded.

05

Non-Ferrous Metal Trading

Trading in aluminum, copper, brass, stainless steel, lead, zinc, and other non-ferrous metals. Precision grading and accurate weight measurement guaranteed.

06

Scrap Sorting & Classification

Professional sorting and classification of mixed scrap by type, alloy, grade, and composition — ensuring maximum market value and compliance with buyer specifications.

07

Metal Scrap Handling

Complete handling of all scrap materials from initial receipt and inspection, through weighing and documentation, to final dispatch. Full chain-of-custody accountability.

08

Industrial Waste Management

Structured collection and responsible disposal of industrial metal waste from manufacturing plants, refineries, and construction projects in compliance with KSA regulations.
