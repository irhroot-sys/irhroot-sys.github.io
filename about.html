Our Story

Who We Are

Amanat Al-Kalima Company (AALKC) was established with a clear purpose: to become the most trusted metal scrap trading and recycling partner in the region. Founded over two decades ago, we have grown from a local scrap dealer into a full-service metal trading company with an active global presence.

We specialise in the collection, sorting, processing, and trade of ferrous and non-ferrous scrap metals. Our facilities are equipped with modern shredding, baling, and shearing machinery to prepare materials to the highest international specifications demanded by steel mills and smelters worldwide.

At AALKC, we believe that responsible recycling and fair commerce go hand in hand. Every transaction is conducted with full transparency, competitive pricing based on live market rates, and a genuine commitment to environmental stewardship.

  • Transparency and integrity in every deal
  • Competitive, market-driven pricing for suppliers
  • Strict environmental and safety compliance
  • Reliable logistics and on-time delivery
