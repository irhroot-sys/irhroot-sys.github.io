Our Story

Who We Are

AMANAT AL-KALIMA COMPANY (شركة أمانة الكلمة) is a scrap trading company registered and operating in Dammam, Eastern Province, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

We are engaged in the buying, selling, collection, handling, sorting, and trading of scrap materials. We coordinate professionally with clients, suppliers, buyers, and local market participants across Dammam and the Eastern Province.

Our business identity is built on the principles of trust, professionalism, and accountability. Every transaction we conduct reflects our commitment to straightforward, honest, and reliable business dealing.

  • Registered company in Dammam, Eastern Province, Saudi Arabia
  • Based in Dammam, Eastern Province, Saudi Arabia
  • Specialised in scrap material trading and related activities
  • Professional coordination with local and regional market participants
